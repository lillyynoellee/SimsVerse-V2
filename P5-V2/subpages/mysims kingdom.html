MySims Kingdom
MySims Kingdom is the second game in the MySims series. It was developed by EA Redwood Shores and published by Electronic Arts (EA) on October 28, 2008. In it, the player, along with sidekicks Buddy and Lyndsay, must help King Roland by traveling to various other islands in the kingdom and restoring them with the power the Royal Wandolier possesses. The Royal Wandolier plays the same role as they did in the previous game, utilizing their power of essences and building to bring life back to the kingdom.

Gameplay

The player starts off on Capital Island where a contest for the next Royal Wandolier ensues, during this the player will go through a tutorial to show how the game is played. Afterwards the Player is bestowed the honor of becoming the Royal Wandolier, to which they use their wand (hence the title, wandolier) and gather essences to complete ‘Scrolls’ which unlock new items you can use to decorate various islands. Although, in order to place these items you need a certain amount of “Mana” for each you decide to use. Mana is sort of like a currency which you can obtain through activities like mining. The kingdom consists of 11 various islands you can adventure through and reconstruct, including the starting island, “Capital Island”.

Platforms

MySims Kingdom is a cross-platform game that can be found on Nintendo Wii and DS. In the Wii version of the game, you are a simple townsfolk who happens to earn the role as Royal Wandolier to go off and restore the other islands in the kingdom. In the DS version, you are a townsfolk who comes back from vacation, and during the night a mysterious figure comes and destroys the town. It is your goal to restore the progress back to the kingdom and take down the unknown villain.
