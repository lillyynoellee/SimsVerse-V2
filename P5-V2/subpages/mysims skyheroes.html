MySims Skyheroes
MySims Skyheroes is the sixth and final installment in the MySims Series. It was developed by Behavior Interactive and published by Electronic Arts (EA) on September 28, 2010. In it, the setting is based in the skies, utilizing the aesthetic of aircrafts. The player must lead the resistance in order to stop Morcubus and his drone army from taking over the skyways. Customize your plane with wings and engines obtained from finishing missions, or play in different modes such as solo campaign and boss battles, and local co-op or online multiplayer!

Platforms

MySims is cross-platform and can be found on Nintendo Wii, DS, XBOX 360, and PS3. Story-wise, the game has no real difference between each version. Although gameplay wise a few things vary. The DS version focuses on missions which are given by the many different characters in the game. These missions are essential, as they are the only way to progress through the game. In the console versions (Wii/Xbox/PS3) however, they take on more of a racing/fps sort of aspect rather than the task system. You take on missions (which are more time-based and fps centered) in different places like tropics with volcanoes to cityscapes. You can use power-ups to increase your speed, alter your projectiles, etc.
