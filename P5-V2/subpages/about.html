History & Development

MySims was developed by Visceral Games (formally known as EA Redwood Shores) and published under its parent company, EA. About 242 developers and a total team of 334 people worked on the game. The music and audio for the game was composed by John Enroth and Silas Hite of Mutato Muzika. The tracks differentiate from each building/area the player is nearby. Emmy Toyonaga who was credited as the lead concept for the MySims franchise, claims that the whole idea was for the characters to have a more chibi looking design, short and stubby, much like many of Nintendoâ€™s well known mascots like Mario and Kirby.

Profits

While the exact profits EA had gained from the release of the first MySims game are unknown, it is estimated that the game had sold over 53 million copies worldwide.
