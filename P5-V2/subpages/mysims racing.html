MySims Racing
MySims Racing is the fourth game in the MySims series which was developed by a different studio, Artificial Mind & Movement and published by EA on June 12, 2009. In MySims Racing, Speedville’s former champion has mysteriously disappeared in flight, leaving the town desolate. This leaves the perfect opportunity for local bad guy Morcubus to take over and build his master company, MorcuCorp. Learn the ropes and win championships in order to stop Morcubus!

Gameplay

The player starts out as a character who moves to Speedville, which had been previously abandoned due to all the citizens and the former champion disappearing. The player is eager to learn how to become a better racer and Ol’ Gabby helps them practice. Eventually the player gets to participate in championships. And while the game itself is reminiscent of other racing games like Mario Kart, it has some special features that allow you to customize your character and kart. There’s also a story-mode for the game. Quick race mode is another mode in which the player is put against 5 other CPUs to race around obstacles on a track and complete “tasks‘. These tasks will reward the player with different customization options for their car.

Platforms

MySims Racing is a cross-platform game that can be found on Nintendo Wii and DS. MySims Racing Wii in comparison to its DS counterpart is almost identical. Gameplay wise, everything is the same with the exception of the DS online-play multiplayer feature. Appearance wise it is also a bit different.
