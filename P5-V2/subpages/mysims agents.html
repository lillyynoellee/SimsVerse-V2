MySims Agents
MySims Agents is the fifth installment in the MySims Series, which was developed by EA Redwood Shores for the Wii, and TOSE for the DS. It was released by Electronic Arts (EA) in Europe on September 25, 2009 and later released in North America on September 29. 2009. In it, you play as an agent who is given tasks to complete and a team to recruit which help you uncover and take down the evil Morcubus’ plans.

Gameplay

During the game, you are accepted as a junior agent for a special agent company and are given your own headquarters with five floors you can decorate, as well as a jet. Throughout the game you are required to complete tasks such as following footprints, picking locks, hacking computers, and more in order to get closer to your goal of stopping Morcubus. You are given various gadgets which help you complete these tasks, and many different areas you explore to investigate.

Platforms

MySims is cross-platform and can be found on Nintendo Wii and DS. While in the Wii version the main objective is to take down Morcubus and his company MorcuCorp, in the DS version the plot is slightly different as the player is instructed to track down the infamous Thief V from stealing the town’s ancient treasure. In order to do this, the player must travel to other locations and instead of completing tasks, you must play through various minigames and side missions. The DS version also brings back small little features like the use of essences.
