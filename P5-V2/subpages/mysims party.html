MySims Party
MySims Party is the third game in the MySims series. It was developed by EA Redwood Shores and published by Electronic Arts (EA) on March 10, 2009. In it, the player is tasked with playing and winning festivals in order to attract new people into a rundown town in order to bring life to it.

Gameplay

The player starts in their town which starts out relatively empty. The player must participate in festivals hosted by MC Emi and Mitch in order to unlock more residents. There are 12 differently themed festivals and over 50 minigames. As a result of winning these festivals, the player unlocks different decorations for their house and more options to customize their sim. As well, when a player achieves a certain amount of points for a characterâ€™s festival, that specific character will move into town. Before participating in a festival, the player must choose from four different buffs, which are helpful for assisting in minigames. Certain minigames will require certain buffs which give the player an advantage over other competitors.

Platforms

MySims Party is a cross-platform game that can be found on Nintendo Wii and DS. In the Wii version, the player competes in various festivals to pull characters into town. The DS version plays out basically the exact same way, the only difference is the DS version focuses more on the star system to progress through the rest of the game. With the star rank system, the player relies on the completion of festivals to earn stars, which are used to unlock more minigames, items, and specific areas of the town.
